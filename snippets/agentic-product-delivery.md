# Agentic Product Delivery

## Pattern

Break product development into layers that can be delegated without losing product intent.

- Human defines the user problem, success criteria, and risk boundaries.
- UI agent accelerates interaction ideas and frontend scaffolding.
- Coding agent handles implementation, cleanup, integration, and refactoring.
- Retrieval or research agents gather supporting information where the product depends on external data.
- Human evals decide whether the output is actually useful before automation is expanded.

## Why It Works

This approach keeps AI from becoming a vague productivity claim. Each agent has a role, a boundary, and an evaluation point.
